Directions

Where To Go

The wedding and reception will be held at Brandywine Valley Baptist Church in Wilmington, DE. The ceremony will be at 2pm followed by a dessert reception in the church fellowship hall.

How to get there

From points south:

Take I-95 North to exit 8 for US-202 North. Keep left at the exit and continue on US-202 N for 3.5 miles. After the Concord Gallery shopping center on your left, you'll see a sign for Mt. Lebanon Rd; turn left there. The church is 1/4 mile ahead on the right.

From points north:

Take I-95 South to exit 8B for US-202 North. Continue on US-202 N for 2 miles. After the Concord Gallery shopping center on your left, you'll see a sign for Mt. Lebanon Rd; turn left there. The church is 1/4 mile ahead on the right.
